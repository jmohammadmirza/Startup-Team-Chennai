Meet the Kobster Team

Vineet Neeraj Co-Founder Kobster

An engineer by education but an entrepreneur by passion,Vineet has close to 3 years of corporate experience which includes working in two startups before embarking on his own venture. His ambition is to create employment opportunities in India. He loves working with energetic and like-minded individuals. His interests include Sales, Marketing, Fund raising, Hiring, Customer Service, Strategy and everything else under the Sun that an entrepreneur should do.

Karthik Co-Founder Kobster

Karthik started his carrier at Cognizant Technology Solutions in 2009, and after 3 years of software development,he fell prey to the entrepreneurship bug & started his venture Kobster.com which aims to consumarize the B2B shopping experience in India. Technology, Finance & Supply Chain is where he contributes the most to Kobster, and he is also involved closely in innovative measures to improve the customer experience.

Mohan Gayam Co-Founder Kobster

Mohan Gayam handles the Supply Chain activities at Kobster. He strives to improve the product catalogue and provide a hassle-free experience to Kobster’s customers. His dream is to expand operations to all metropolitan cities and gather a definite market share in the office-supplies segment. Prior to co-founding Kobster, Mohan worked with HCL. He loves traveling and reading fantasy books.

About us

Kobster is a technology-led office supplies company offering complete solutions to procure, manage, analyse and optimise office supplies for a corporate. Kobster.com aims to consumarize the corporate procurement process and revolutionize the way corporates shop for their office needs by leveraging the power of e-commerce .
