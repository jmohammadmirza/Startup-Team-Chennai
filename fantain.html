Meet the Fantain Team

Anand Ramachandran CEO Fantain.

Anand is a business development professional from the IT Services Industry with experience selling complex transformation solutions to Fortune 500 companies in India and the US.   He overseas Fantain's overall strategy, marketing and finance .  Anand loves to use his passion for technology and sport for building a world class company that truly benefits sports fans.

Arvind Ramachandran Chief Product Officer Fantain.

Aravind has 20 years of experience in software products and IT services industries. He leads an enthusiastic bunch of people and builds the Fantain platform. He is also the Product Manager for Cricket @ Fantain. Aravind is a visionary who imagines how fans can engage more pervasively with the sport they love and strives to convert that vision into great products.

Vivek Venugopalan CTO Fantain.

Vivek makes the primary choices and decisions around technologies used at Fantain. He is a technology advisor and decision maker for various clients around the globe in the Retail, CPG and transportation industry. He has 20+ years of experience in the IT industry, helping clients determine the best technology solutions, to drive their business efficiently. His expertise and interests lie in Retail Technologies, E-Commerce, Multi Channel Retailing, Large scale distributed systems, Cloud and Grid computing.
