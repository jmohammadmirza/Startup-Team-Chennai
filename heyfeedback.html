About us

HeyFeedback is an online survey creation tool that allows you to create a survey online and distribute it to your audience to let them respond online across any device - desktop, laptop, tablets or smartphone. Even if you need to gather data offline without internet connectivity, the HeyFeedback android app lets you collect responses and sync back when you are online again. If you can use PowerPoint or Keynote, you can be an instant expert !
